Morning Prayer


I. INVOCATION

V    O Lord open our lips;
R    and our mouths shall proclaim your praise.


Glory to the Father, and the Son, and to the Holy Spirit
as it was in the beginning, is now, and shall be forever. AMEN

Now go to Advent, Christmas, Lent, Easter, Ascension to Pentecost, (Other Days) Option 1, Option 2, Option 3 or Option 4, according to the season.
