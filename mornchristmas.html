Christmas (Incarnation)

Psalm 98

  1. O Sing to the Lord a new song:
    for he has done marvellous things.

  2. his right hand and his holy arm:
    they have got him the victory.

  3. The Lord has made known his salvation:
    he has revealed his just deliverance in the sight of all the
    nations.

  4. He has remembered his mercy and faithfulness
    towards the house of Israel:
    and all the ends of the earth have seen
    the salvation of our God.

  5. Shout with joy to the Lord, all the earth:
    break into singing and make melody.

  6. For he has come to judge the earth:
    he shall judge the world with righteousness,
         and the peoples with equity.

  7. Glory to the Father...

In many and various ways God spoke of old to our fathers by the
prophets; but in these last days he has spoken to us by a Son, whom
he has appointed the heir of all things, through whom he has also
created the world. (Heb. 1.1-2)
