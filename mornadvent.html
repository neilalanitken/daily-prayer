II.     PSALMODY AND SCRIPTURE


Advent (Anticipation)

Psalm 97

  1. The Lord Is King; let the earth rejoice:
    let the multitude of islands be glad.

  2. Clouds and Darkness are round him;
    righteousness and justice are the foundations of his throne.

  3. For you, Lord, are the most high over all the earth:
    you are exalted far above all gods.

  4. The Lord loves those that hate evil:
    the Lord guards the life of the faithful, and delivers them
    from the hands of the ungodly.

  5. Light dawns for the righteous:
    and joy for the true of heart.

  6. Rejoice in the Lord, you righteous:
    and give thanks to his holy name.

  7. Glory to the Father...

Besides this you know what hour it is, how it is full time now for you
to wake from sleep. For Salvation is nearer to us now than when we
first believed. (Rom. 13.11-12)
